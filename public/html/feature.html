Story

Arts


Aesthetically we want our game to look like a monotonous office to contrast with the absurdity of the main character’s task. Moreover, it makes it easy for the player to distinguish between enemies/interactable objects and decorations, as the interactable objects will be brighter, and the background will be duller. We drew inspiration from TV shows like “The Office”, which showcase a generic office space.




Our main character has a cartoonish design. The main character model will be a meek looking businessman holding a briefcase as his weapon.


Weapon


A core feature of our game is that you only have one main weapon, so that every time you throw it you'd like to get it back.


The weapon is be modelled as a briefcase. Although the player is limited to only one weapon, this weapon can be upgraded to possess various mechanics. The upgrades currently are homing, size/damage increase and boomerang throw.
